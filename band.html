ABOUT THE BLANKS

The Blanks is an Indie Alternative Band from Las Vegas. From open mics to youtube hits, from local venues to music festivals, the Blanks have come a long way since our humble beginnings in Las Vegas. The journey has been a long and a very memorable one. We have been through a lot as a band and we wouldn’t have made it if not for our devoted fans who has been there for us. As a band, we just want to spread the music that inspire us to inspire other too.

To make people feel that they are heard, that they are not invisible, that thought our music, they can have a home. This concert is to show our gratitude for all the fans that have been with us all the way. All attendees for the concert will be able to get exclusive merchandize as our thank you for all the great support. We guarantee an amazing show and again we would just like to thank everyone for all the support that led to our success.
